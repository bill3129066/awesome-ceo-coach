## OKR Rules (Mochary Ch.20 + Bailey)
- OKRs are set collaboratively — not top-down, not bottom-up
- Each person should have 2-3 objectives max, each with 2-3 key results
- Key results must be measurable and have a due date
- 70% achievement = well-calibrated stretch goal
- Review weekly (brief), score quarterly
- Counter-metrics: for every KR, identify what could go wrong if you over-optimize (Mochary Ch.23)
